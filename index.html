Digital Integrated Circuit/VLSI

Cadence Virtuoso

With current technology, a computer microship contains hundreds of thousands of transistor, hence Very Large Integrated Circui, VLSI. There are many hierarchies and levels of abstractionin VLSI design. They range from high-level system design to transistor-level circuit layout. This project require creation of structural and functional descriptions of elementary and complex logic functions in a hierarchical system and use Verilog simulations to verify functional behavior. When it is done, the circuit layout is drawn and stimulated for correct implementation.

Our team is to design an ALU which preforms arithmetic and logic operations on numbers, e.g. AND, OR, ADD, Subtract, Increment, Decrement, Compare, etc. The minimum operations expected are: Add, Add with carry, Subtract, Subtract with borrow, Negate (2's complement), Logical AND, Logical OR, Logical XOR, Complement (1's complement), Compare (set on less than, set on larger than, set on equal, set on less than or equal), Arithmetic Shift Left, and Rotate Left. Minimum number of bits is 4 bits. Clock frequency ≥ 100 MHz.

Full details can be found on the VLSI project

Intrabody Communication

PCB design

I am assiting a PHD student in his Intrabody Communication project.

Picture above shows the board of which we wanted to build. The mixer used on the RX board is the AD8348 quadrature demodulator. The received signal from the EF IBC channel is fed to the RF input of the mixer and a local oscillator signal that is generated by an AD9954 DDS is fed to the LO inputs. The received signal is first amplified by an RF band low-noise amplifier with 19dB-gain and mixed to baseband. A baseband 4th-order butterworth low-pass filter with 3dB bandwidth at 6MHz is used to eliminate the high-frequency harmonics and carriers. The low-pass filtered signal is then amplified by an AD8369 digitally controlled variable gain amplifier (VGA). An AD9201 dual channel, 20MHz, 10-bit resolution CMOS ADC is placed after the VGA to quantize the amplified baseband signal. The ADC input differential dynamic range is ±1V. The gain of the VGA on the RX board is set to different values to ensure that the input voltage to the ADC does not saturate, since the TX cannot adjust its output voltage. The ADC quantized data are fed to an SN65LV1023A parallel-to-serial converter and then the serialized data are forwarded to an HFBR-53D5 fiber optical transceiver. The RX board noise floor is at -140dBm/Hz.

Data are transmitted through an optical fiber to the optical transceiver on another PCB, on which the optical signal is converted back to serial electrical data and deserialized by an SN65LV1224B deserializer. The parallel output of the deserializer is recorded by an Agilent 16902B logic analyzer

Heart Rate Monitor

Microcontroller, Sensor, real-time display

First, photo diode sensor senses the pulse signal from a finger tip. Then the sensor output is read by the Arduino board, which then transfers the data to the PC through a serial interface. A PC application has been developed using C language and running on Processing program to calculate instantaneous heart rate and display it.

Wifi part - Once our PC application finishes the calculation, it will send the heart rate value to an android phone over Wi-Fi. We developed an Android application in our HUAWEI phone and it will receive the data of heart rate from our PC. And the data would be processed in the Android application, therefore the phone would give alarm if the heart rate is out of normal range (in our case, 50 - 120 BPM).

Bluetooth part - Once our PC application finishes the calculation, it will send the heart rate value to our Arduino board through serial port. Then the Arduino board will send the beat rate to our BlueTooth module, which will transmit the data to an Android phone. A self-built app in our LG phone will receive the data of heart rate through “BlueInput” app and process the data in the app. The phone would give alarm if the heart rate is out of normal range (in our case, 50 - 120 BPM).

Access Network Engineer

Huawei Technologies

The job responsility is to deal with the telecommunication equipment above, which is called IPSLAM (Internet Protocol Digital Subscriber Line Access Multiplexer). The internet and landline telephone service that go out from your house will get connected into this IPDSLAM, so that you can access the network and connect to the world. It is basically an UNIX system!

An IP DSLAM was the first pairing of a DSLAM with either an IP card that assists with the management and operation of the multiple service access traffic associated with subscribe lines up to the notion of an integrated on board interface that was expressly proving IP for uplink and often with some (limited) IP routing capabilities.

Our team is responsible to install and maintain this equipment. Other words, whenver users have problem on internet and telephone, we have to solve their problems.
